Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Mall of Travels respects the privacy of its users and complies with data protection laws, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for European residents, the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and the specific laws of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania regarding privacy and personal data protection.

2. Types of Data Collected

We collect several types of personal data when you use our Site:

  • Identification Data : Name, surname, email address, phone number, mailing address, and travel information.
  • Financial Data : Credit card information and other payment methods.
  • Technical Data : IP address, browser type, operating system, and device information.
  • Location Data : Geographical coordinates if you have enabled location sharing.
  • Booking Data : Information about the services booked, including travel dates, selected services, and booking history.

3. Use of Data

We use your personal data for the following purposes:

  • Booking Processing : Confirming bookings, managing payments, and providing customer support.
  • Service Improvement : Personalizing your experience on the Site, analyzing browsing behavior to improve our services.
  • Marketing and Communication : Sending promotional offers, newsletters, and information about services that may interest you, based on your preferences.
  • Legal Compliance : Complying with tax and security laws, including fraud prevention.

4. Sharing Data

We may share your personal data with the following entities:

  • Service Providers : Our partners and service providers (hotels, airlines, car rental agencies) for the execution of the services you have booked.
  • Marketing Partners : If you have consented to receive promotional communications, we may share your data with our marketing partners.
  • Regulatory Authorities : In compliance with legal requirements, we may share your information with tax, legal, or administrative authorities.

5. Data Security

Mall of Travels implements appropriate security measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. All financial information is encrypted using SSL (Secure Socket Layer) protocols.

6. Your Rights

In accordance with applicable laws, you have several rights concerning your personal data:

  • Right of Access : You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification : You can correct any inaccurate or incomplete personal data.
  • Right to Erasure : You can request the deletion of your personal data in certain cases.
  • Right to Object : You can object to the use of your data for marketing or profiling purposes.
  • Right to Data Portability : You can request to receive your personal data in a structured, commonly used format.

7. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, personalize content, and analyze interactions with our Site. You have the option to manage your cookie preferences in your browser settings. However, some features of the Site may not function properly if you disable cookies.

8. Data Retention

We will retain your personal data for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected or until you exercise your right to erasure, subject to legal retention obligations.

9. Changes to the Privacy Policy

Mall of Travels reserves the right to modify this privacy policy at any time. Any modifications will be posted on this page and will take effect as soon as they are published.

10. Contact

If you have any questions or wish to exercise your rights regarding your personal data, you can contact us at the following address: https://www.malloftravels.com/contact/
