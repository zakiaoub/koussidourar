Cookies

1. Introduction

Our website uses cookies and other trackers to enhance your online experience, personalize content, provide targeted advertisements, and analyze traffic. This policy explains which cookies are used, for what purposes, and how you can manage your preferences.

2. What is a cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. It stores information about your browsing and sends it back to the site on your future visits, for example to remember your preferences or language settings.

3. Your cookie choices

You can configure your browser to accept, block, or delete cookies. Here are links to the official guides for the most common browsers:

  • Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/
  • Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/
  • Safari: https://support.apple.com/safari
  • Edge: https://support.microsoft.com/edge

4. Sharing and use of your data

We may share certain anonymized information with our business and technology partners to:

  • Personalize your experience
  • Provide advertising services
  • Improve our products and services

We do not share your sensitive personal information without your explicit consent.

5. Consequences of sharing your device

If multiple people use the same device or browser, cookies will reflect the browsing of the active user. We cannot guarantee that recommendations or advertisements will be personalized for each individual user in this case.
